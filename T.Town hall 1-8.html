On town hall 1-8 you are only learning how to play. The first important defensive buildings are mortars and air defense. Later, the magic tower and tesla appears.

Also gradually appear important buildings without which it is impossible to play normally: a laboratory, a spell factory, more barracks and camps.

A very important stage in the game is the appearance of the black elixir, which opens the dark spell factory, the dark barracks, the dark warriors, the storage of the black elixir, its mining stations, and most importantly, the heroes!

New features are also gradually appearing: Clans, Clan Wars, Clan Games, Clan Wars League, Clan Capital, Builder Base, Shop, Seasonal Tasks. You can read about much of this in my other posts.

Heroes are very important for battles and they consume dark elixir. Your first hero is the Barbarian King.

Low leveled town halls are funny to play but there is a feeling that the studies are not over and you are a little limited.
